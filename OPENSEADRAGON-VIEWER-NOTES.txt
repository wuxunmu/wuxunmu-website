OpenSeadragon Direct Hires Viewer v5

This version is intended for GitHub Pages deployment without DZI tiles.

Important:
- It directly loads w.hires_image from data/artworks.json.
- It does not generate or require images/artworks/dzi/.
- OpenSeadragon is bundled locally at js/vendor/openseadragon.min.js.
- artworks.html no longer depends on the jsDelivr CDN.
- The left "return to artworks" button is visible again.
- The left "return to artworks" button now follows the current site language:
  English shows "Back to artworks"; Chinese shows "返回作品页面".
- Viewer loading/status messages now follow the current site language too:
  English pages show English messages; Chinese pages show Chinese messages.
- The viewer follows the page language first, then falls back to the saved preference,
  then to the visitor's browser/system language.
- Artworks page now presents "Thresholds of Time and Space" as the current series
  and links to a separate More Works page for future additional works.
- The top-right close button remains visible on mobile.
- Chinese status text in the OpenSeadragon overlay has been corrected.
- Double tap now uses a custom detail zoom and resets with viewport.goHome() instead of shrinking around the tapped point.
- Double tap uses viewport.pointFromPixel() to avoid sending the image toward a corner.

Test:
1. Deploy.
2. Open /artworks.html in a fresh/private browser session.
3. Tap an artwork.
4. Pinch with two fingers to zoom and drag to inspect details.
5. Use the top-right close button to return to the page.
