Direct Hires Viewer v6

This version loads the 6000px /images/artworks/hires/*-hires.jpg directly when an artwork is opened.

How to verify:
1. Deploy to Cloudflare.
2. Open artworks page in an incognito/private window.
3. Tap/click one artwork.
4. The zoom label should briefly show "高清加载中", then "100%".
5. Right click/open image in new tab on desktop, or inspect the image URL: it should contain /images/artworks/hires/ and -hires.jpg?v=hires-v5.
6. On mobile, double tap a detail area: it jumps to 300%. Pinch with two fingers to continue zooming.
